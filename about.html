My Resume

...myself

My name is Céline Cosneau-Ampis, I am 30 years old and less than 2 years ago I became a UX designer after working 3 years in the service sector.

I’ve always loved solving problems and being a UX Designer enables me to find solutions while using my creativity. Also, the human-centered approach of UX is what comforted my new career choice : putting the end-user at the center of design decision makes and gives sense to every project.

I’ve followed a first UX Design training via CareerFoundry thanks to which I’ve learnt about the UX Design process, Design Thinking and how to use tools such as Adobe XD or Invision.
Today, I am enrolled in a UX/UI designer Bachelor in apprenticeship to further my knowledge and skills. Besides, I am currently looking for a company for which my skills will benefit to.

...my values

🌏 Open-minded

Travelling and living abroad opened my eyes on how behaviors are influenced by one’s culture, but also how richer interactions with people can result from it.

📈 Progress

« No one is perfect ». I believe that there is always place for improvement and learning something new. Which is why I find UX and UI Designs so thrilling!

🤝 Respect

Learning about Accessibility Design made me realise how the digital world can be exclusive. I want to design projects that respect and embrace differences, that are all-inclusive and do not leave anyone feel excluded.
